Binary SearchAlgorithms are just as important as the structures that hold the data. Let's look at Binary Search.If you have a sorted array and you want to find a specific number, you could check every single item one by one (Linear Search, $O(n)$ time). But Binary Search is much faster ($O(\log n)$ time). It works by repeatedly dividing the search interval in half—just like finding a word in a physical dictionary.
